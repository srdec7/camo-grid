Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I bought the "Remove Ads" pass, but ads are still showing. What should I do?
A. Please open the game's Shop Menu and tap the "Restore Purchases" button at the bottom. StoreKit will securely query your Apple ID and re-activate your ad-free status immediately.
Q. My game move count did not increase after watching an ad.
A. Make sure your device has a stable internet connection. If the ad fails to play completely or gets cut off early, the game might not receive the completion callback. Try watching the ad fully before closing the prompt.
Q. How do I request a refund for a purchase?
A. All billing transactions are managed directly by Apple. To request a refund, please sign into Apple's Report a Problem page with your Apple ID and select Request a Refund.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share while playing CAMO Grid.

1. Information We Collect

CAMO Grid does not require or collect any personal identity information (such as your name, phone number, or home address) to play.

  • Device & Identifier Data: We may access anonymous device identifiers (such as Apple’s IDFA or Google's GAID) solely to serve and measure advertisements.
  • In-App Purchase Receipts: Transactions are handled directly by the App Store. We only check local purchase status to verify and unlock your Purchased Items (e.g. No-Ads status).

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect data used to identify you in accordance with their own policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used to serve advertisements in the game. Google AdMob may collect and use device identifiers, geolocation data, and ad interaction logs to personalize ads. You can review Google's privacy terms here.
  • Apple StoreKit: Handles processing of native payments and purchase receipts.

3. Children's Privacy

Our game is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe your child has shared data with us, please contact us at the support address below.
